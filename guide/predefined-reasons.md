# Predefined Moderation Reasons

This page lists all official predefined moderation reasons used by staff.  
Each entry includes the **alias** and the corresponding **reason message**.

## Tickets & Requests

### `ticket`
Leaving tickets empty or failing to respond wastes staff time. Please only create a ticket if you’re sure about your request. Multiple violations will result in a ban.

### `requestping`
Please don’t ping staff for requests. Requests are fulfilled based on availability, and upvotes do not guarantee that the request will be leaked.

### `outsidereq`
Requests are only allowed in our request channels. Posting them elsewhere isn’t allowed. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>. You can create a request here: https://requests.6ureleaks.com.

## Promotion & Advertising

### `selfpromo`
Self-promotion is only allowed in <#1430412012743037058>. Promoting your socials elsewhere is not allowed. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `payhippromo`
Promoting your payhip elsewhere is not allowed. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `doublead`
Please don't post your ad more than once. You can find our rules pinned in <#1310079315227967588>.

### `dmad`
DM advertising is not allowed. You are banned as a result.

## Piracy & Accounts

### `piracy`
Asking for pirated content (e.g. cracked software, full movies/shows) is not allowed and goes against Discord’s Terms of Service. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `sendingpiracy`
Sending pirated content (e.g. cracked software, full movies/shows) is not allowed and goes against Discord’s Terms of Service. If you do this again, you will be banned. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `accounts`
Selling or Buying accounts is against our <#1250087429763760250> and Discord's ToS.

## NSFW

### `nsfw`
Please stop sending NSFW or inappropriate content. Doing this again will result in a permanent ban. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `nsfwban`
Posting NSFW content in non-age-restricted channels violates Discord’s Terms of Service. You are banned as a result.

## Scamming & Fraud

### `tradescam`
Scamming in trades is not tolerated. You are banned as a result. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `hacked`
Banned for sending phishing links or harmful files. If your account was hacked, please secure it and appeal for an unban.

## Premium & Protection

### `premiumban`
Sharing Premium-exclusive presets with non-Premium members, inside or outside this server, is strictly prohibited. This includes redistributing files, or sending them privately. You are banned as a result.

### `premiumrequest`
Requesting Premium-exclusive presets for free or attempting to bypass Premium access is not allowed. Further violations will result in suspension or removal from the server.

### `password`
Sharing password access to our preset files outside of the server or within is strictly prohibited. You are banned as a result.

### `protectedrequest`
Requesting content from protected users that is restricted within this server is strictly prohibited. Respect user protection rules. Further action has been taken.

### `protectedshare`
Sharing, redistributing, or asking others to share content from protected users is not allowed. This includes sending it privately or posting it elsewhere.

## Spam, Staff & Authority

### `channelspam`
Please refrain from spamming the same message in multiple channels, or you will get kicked. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `ignoringstaff`
Failure to comply with staff instructions is not allowed. Please cooperate with staff to avoid escalation.

### `escalating`
You were instructed to stop arguing and continued anyway. Disregarding staff instructions and escalating conflict is not tolerated. Further action has been taken.

### `disputestaff`
Publicly arguing or attempting to undermine staff decisions in chat is not permitted. If you have concerns, use the proper appeal or support channels.

### `pingingstaff`
Pinging staff unnecessarily or repeatedly is not allowed. Further violations will result in a timeout or mute.

### `disrespectstaff`
Disrespectful behavior toward staff, including sarcasm or public ridicule, is not acceptable. Continued behavior will result in further action.

## Harassment & Toxicity

### `harassment`
Harassment, targeting, or repeatedly bothering members is not tolerated. Please remain respectful and read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `instigating`
Starting arguments, provoking members, or creating unnecessary conflict is not tolerated. This behavior disrupts the community. Further violations will result in action.

### `toxicity`
Excessive toxicity, instigating arguments, or disruptive behavior is not tolerated. Continued behavior will result in further action.

### `derogatory`
Use of derogatory, degrading, or insulting language toward other members is not tolerated. Keep discussions respectful. Further violations will result in action.

### `threat`
Threatening other members, including staff, is strictly prohibited. Any form of intimidation or harmful intent will result in immediate disciplinary action.

### `hatespeech`
Hate speech of any kind is not tolerated and is against Discord’s Terms of Service. Please read our <#1250087429763760250>.

## Severe Violations

### `vcslur`
Use of racial slurs in voice chat is strictly prohibited and violates Discord’s Terms of Service. Hate speech of any kind will result in an immediate ban.

### `racism`
Racism of any kind is strictly prohibited and violates both our rules and Discord’s Terms of Service. Please remain respectful & read our <#1250087429763760250>.

### `doxxing`
Sharing personal information (doxxing) is strictly prohibited and results in an instant ban.

### `raiding`
Coordinated raids or spamming with others is not allowed. You are banned as a result.

### `impersonation`
Pretending to be staff or another member is not tolerated. Please stop or you will be banned.

### `alts`
Using alternate accounts to bypass bans or punishments is prohibited. You are banned as a result.

## Legal

### `legalthreat`
Threatening legal action in direct messages or in public is inappropriate. All content removal requests must be submitted through our official DMCA form: https://requests.6ureleaks.com/dmca. Continued harassment will result in further action.

## Intellectual Property

### `plagiarism`
Plagiarism, including claiming or distributing another editor’s work as your own, is strictly prohibited. Action has been taken.

### `hypocrisy`
Using or benefiting from leaks while publicly opposing them is considered hypocrisy and will result in a ban.
